## Writing Guidelines for Future Posts

### Tone & Voice
- Professional yet compassionate
- Research-backed but accessible
- Hopeful without being dismissive of pain
- Direct and honest about challenges

### Structure
- Clear H2 and H3 headings
- Data points and statistics bolded
- Practical takeaways in each section
- Bullet lists for readability
- Tables where appropriate

### Data Integration
- Include specific percentages and statistics
- Reference studies by author and year
- Explain *why* the data matters to readers
- Balance research with real-world application

### SEO Optimization
- Target long-tail keywords (e.g., "how long does affair recovery take")
- Include location for local SEO (San Diego)
- Answer questions people actually search
- Link internally to related content
